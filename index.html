Our Story

About Umoja
P.A.G Church

We are a vibrant church in Umoja, Nairobi, dedicated to sharing the love of Christ and building a strong community of faith.

Through worship, prayer, and the Word of God, we create a welcoming space where everyone can grow, connect, and find purpose.

Our Core Values

We are building a church marked by God's presence, deep belonging, and visible love that reaches beyond Sunday.

01
Prayer + Presence

Spirit-Led Worship

We gather expecting God to move. Prayer, Scripture, and wholehearted worship shape how we listen, lead, and respond to His voice.

PrayerScriptureWorship
02
Belonging + Growth

Covenant Community

We do not build a crowd. We build a family where people are known, discipled, supported, and challenged to grow together in Christ.

FellowshipCareDiscipleship
03
Love + Mission

Compassion in Action
